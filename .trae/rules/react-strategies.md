description: When writing any react.js project
---

1. All projects must have a Modules folder.
2. Modules folder may have subfolders like "Components", "Services", "Views", "Utils", "Context", "Models", "Hooks".
3. The output of a Module is the View (JSX) or the Service (function) or the Model (class) or the Context (Provider and Consumer) or the Hook (custom hook).
